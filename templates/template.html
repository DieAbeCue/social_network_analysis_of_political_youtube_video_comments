Bias detection model

In the first space, write one or multiple opinions on one of the topics studied by the model: abortion, gun control or affirmative action. If multiple opinions, separate them with a semi-colon (;).
In the second space, mark the topic of the opinions, (0 for abortion, 1 for gun control, 2 for affirmative action or 3 for unrelated).
If the opinion doesn't come from a political youtube video, write 3 in the third and fourth spaces. Otherwise, input the leaning of the source that published the video under which the comment came, as classified by AllSides.com (refer to the image below for some sources, 0 for left leaning, 1 for center and 2 for right leaning).
In the fourth space, mark the topic of the video the comment came from (0 for abortion, 1 for affirmative action, 2 for gun control, 3 if not applicable).
All inputs must have the same amount of values separated by a semi-colon.
